# Code of Conduct

LastLight is an open-source research project. Contributors are expected to communicate with respect, patience, and good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent when possible.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.
- Welcome beginners and domain experts alike.
- Treat safety-sensitive knowledge with care.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Knowingly adding fabricated or unsafe information.
- Publishing private information without consent.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.
