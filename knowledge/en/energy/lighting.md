Use the lowest light level that is safe for the task. Headlamps are efficient because they aim light where needed and leave hands free. Prefer LED lights over incandescent bulbs when available.

Preserve night vision by using dim light and avoiding unnecessary bright flashes. Keep one light source in a known location so it can be found in darkness.

Candles create fire risk. If candles must be used, place them on stable nonflammable surfaces, away from bedding, curtains, children, pets, and anything that can fall.
