In cold conditions, reduce heat loss by blocking wind, staying dry, insulating from the ground, and wearing loose layers. Wet clothing removes heat quickly.

Use a small safe room if possible and close off unused spaces. Cover drafts around doors and windows without blocking essential ventilation for any combustion appliance.

Never use outdoor grills, charcoal, or fuel stoves indoors for heat. Carbon monoxide can build up without smell or warning.
