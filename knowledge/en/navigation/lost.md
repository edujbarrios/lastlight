If you become lost, stop moving unless staying put is immediately unsafe. Panic walking wastes energy and can move you farther from known routes.

Think through your last confirmed location, observe landmarks, weather, daylight, injuries, and available supplies, then make a simple plan. Mark your location if you must move.

Use visible and audible signals when safe: bright cloth, reflective surfaces, whistle blasts, lights, or ground markings. Conserve battery and energy.
