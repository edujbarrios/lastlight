To extend phone battery life, reduce screen brightness, enable low-power mode, turn off Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, mobile data, location, and background app refresh when not needed. Use airplane mode when communication is not currently required.

Check messages in scheduled batches instead of keeping radios active constantly. Prefer text messages over voice or video. Keep devices warm in cold weather because batteries lose capacity when cold.

If power is scarce, assign one device for communication and keep others off as backups. Write down critical phone numbers and information so the phone does not need to stay on for reference.
