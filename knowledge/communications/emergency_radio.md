Battery, hand-crank, or solar radios can provide official emergency information when phones and internet fail. Use scheduled listening windows to conserve power.

Record important frequencies, times, weather alerts, evacuation routes, and instructions on paper. Share confirmed information calmly and avoid spreading rumors.

Keep radio volume low and use headphones when possible. Store spare batteries with the radio, protected from moisture and extreme heat.
